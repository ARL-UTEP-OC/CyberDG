User Information Guide

This Guide With Help You Navigate the ExLab System.

Exploit Lab (ExLab) is a tool that automatizes the process of testing the effects of malicious software safely and efficiently. ExLab allows users to create scenarios that serve as test environments and to easily create and connect Virtual Machines (VMs) that can serve as attackers or victims.
